Mission

Storix hopes to contribute to environmentally-friendly development. Given humanity’s limitless possibilities, our mission is to assist rapid economic development by ensuring sustainability.

Vision

Storix is inspired and guided by the motto “a little of a lot is still a lot”. As we progress into an age of increased computing demand and natural exploitation, making the most out of our available resources is becoming increasingly important. Through our efforts in enhancing energy efficiency, we hope to increase available energy without further exploitation of our natural environment.

Values

We at Storix value our natural environment and also the contributions of every energy worker. We communicate our respect for them by treasuring the energy they generate through our constant innovations aimed at improving energy efficiency.
